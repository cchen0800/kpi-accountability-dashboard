Project: Agentic KPI Accountability Dashboard

 

Build a small dashboard that shows agentic AI generating synthetic data and flagging/analyzing KPI accountability.

 

Simulate the inputs: Create 5 synthetic "employee" profiles with different writing styles and reporting cadences. Use AI to generate a week of daily status updates from each.
Agentic extraction and analysis: Use an agentic workflow to parse the updates, extract KPIs, and analyze performance across the week. Surface things like submission rate, output volume, quality, and flag accountability gaps. Tooling is your call — n8n, Claude API, Cursor, whatever gets you there.
Dashboard: Build a working dashboard that displays the KPIs and the AI analysis clearly. Doesn't need to be pretty, just needs to be functional.
 

Deliverables:

Working dashboard (deployed link, screen recording, or live demo)
Short writeup: tools used, your approach, what you'd build next with more time
 

We're not evaluating hand-written code. We're looking at how you scope a messy problem, leverage agentic tools, and ship something real.
